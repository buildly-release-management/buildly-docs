Login
=====

Access your Buildly Product Labs workspace securely through our streamlined login process. The platform uses enterprise-grade authentication to protect your data and maintain team security.

Login Process
-------------

**Standard Login**
  1. Navigate to your organization's Buildly Product Labs URL
  2. Enter your registered email address
  3. Enter your password
  4. Click "Sign In" to access your workspace

**First-Time Login**
  After registration, your first login may include:
  
  - Profile completion prompts
  - Team assignment confirmations
  - Platform orientation tour
  - Notification preference setup

Login Interface Features
-----------------------

**Security Features:**
- Secure OAuth authentication
- Session management
- Password complexity requirements
- Failed attempt monitoring

**User Convenience:**
- "Remember Me" option for trusted devices
- Password reset functionality
- Clear error messages for troubleshooting

Authentication Methods
---------------------

**Email/Password**
  Standard authentication using your registered email and password.

**Single Sign-On (SSO)**
  If enabled by your organization:
  
  - Connect through your company's identity provider
  - Use existing corporate credentials
  - Automatic team and permission assignment

Troubleshooting Login Issues
---------------------------

**Forgot Password**
  1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen
  2. Enter your email address
  3. Check your email for reset instructions
  4. Follow the link to create a new password
  5. Return to login with your new credentials

**Account Locked**
  - Contact your system administrator
  - Wait for automatic unlock (if configured)
  - Verify you're using the correct credentials

**Browser Issues**
  - Clear browser cache and cookies
  - Try an incognito/private browsing window
  - Ensure JavaScript is enabled
  - Update your browser to the latest version

**Common Error Messages:**
- "Invalid credentials": Check email and password spelling
- "Account not activated": Complete email verification process
- "Access denied": Contact administrator about permissions

Security Best Practices
-----------------------

**For Users:**
- Use a strong, unique password
- Don't share login credentials
- Log out when using shared computers
- Report suspicious activity immediately

**Password Requirements:**
- Minimum 8 characters
- Mix of uppercase and lowercase letters
- Include numbers and special characters
- Avoid common words or personal information

Session Management
-----------------

**Automatic Features:**
- Sessions expire after periods of inactivity
- Secure logout when browser is closed
- Protection against unauthorized access

**Manual Controls:**
- "Sign Out" to end your session securely
- "Sign Out All Devices" for enhanced security
- Session history in account settings

Getting Help
-----------

If you continue to experience login issues:

- Contact your team administrator
- Check with IT support for SSO-related problems
- Review your organization's specific login procedures
- See :doc:`../troubleshooting/CORS-RESOLUTION-SUMMARY` for technical issues
