Welcome to Buildly Product Labs Documentation

Buildly Product Labs is an AI-powered product management platform designed for modern development teams. This user guide will help you get started with account registration, team collaboration, and utilizing all the platform’s features.

New to Buildly Product Labs? Start with our Getting Started guide for account setup and onboarding.

Platform features and capabilities are explained in detail in the Features section. For comprehensive guidance, check out our complete user documentation.

Note

This documentation is designed for end users of the Buildly Product Labs platform. For developers and technical implementation, visit our open source repositories.
